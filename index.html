Who we are

A community art NGO turning Lebanon's walls into places where people feel seen.

In Lebanon, walls are everywhere — beside schools, along stairways, at the edges of neighborhoods. I Leaf Art believes those walls can do more than divide or decay. Working with artists, volunteers, children, and local partners, we paint murals that gather people, mark shared stories, and give communities something beautiful to call their own.

Why murals matter

A wall can become a place where people feel remembered.

In Lebanon, walls carry daily life. They stand beside schools, streets, stairways, homes, community spaces, and places people pass without noticing. When a wall is painted with care, it can change the feeling of the whole space.

"When a wall is painted with care, it changes the feeling of the whole space."

I Leaf Art believes that public art can soften hard places, invite children to imagine, and remind communities that beauty belongs to them too. The painting process is also part of the impact: people meet, participate, laugh, learn, and leave a visible mark together.
