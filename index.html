Why LeafArt?

Murals can change how a place feels, and who feels invited into it.

LeafArt begins every project by listening. Residents, local artists, schools, small businesses, and civic partners shape each wall before the first brushstroke lands.

Our process turns neglected facades into shared canvases. The finished murals brighten streets, but the deeper work happens in the relationships built while painting them.

We believe public art should be accessible, paid fairly, and rooted in the people who pass it every day.
