Purpose in public

Our Mission

Vision

LeafArt transforms communities through public wall art and murals.

We work with artists, residents, schools, municipalities, and local organizations to create murals that are beautiful, relevant, and lasting. Every project is designed to give communities an active role in shaping the visual identity of their streets.

Our vision is a world where public art is not an occasional luxury, but a common language for care, imagination, remembrance, and civic pride. A wall can become an invitation, a landmark, and a record of shared effort.

Our pillars

Three ways we make impact visible

Empower Artists

We fund paid mural commissions, mentorship, materials, and production support for emerging and established artists.

Transform Spaces

We turn neglected or overlooked walls into vibrant public works that improve daily experience and neighborhood pride.

Build Communities

We host workshops and listening sessions so each mural reflects the people who live, work, and gather nearby.

History

From one wall to a growing movement

2018

LeafArt is founded

A small group of artists and organizers paint the first community mural with donated supplies.

2020

City partnerships begin

LeafArt launches neighborhood mural programs with schools, youth groups, and municipalities.

2023

Artist support fund opens

The NGO creates a dedicated fund for stipends, safety equipment, and public art training.

2025

Regional mural network

LeafArt expands collaborations across 30 cities and builds a network of 500 supported artists.
